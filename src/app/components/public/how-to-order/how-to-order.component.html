Frequently Asked Questions

What is OK Curier?

OK Curier is a reliable courier service provider that ensures your packages are delivered safely and on time.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by navigating to the 'Trimite' section on our website, filling out the necessary details, and submitting the form.

What are the delivery options?

We offer various delivery options including same-day delivery, next-day delivery, and standard delivery based on your needs.

How can I track my package?

You can track your package using the tracking number provided at the time of order. Simply enter the tracking number on our 'Track Order' page.

What should I do if my package is lost or damaged?

If your package is lost or damaged, please contact our customer service immediately with your order details for assistance.
